Common Information About Bermuda

Where is Bermuda Located?

Off of the east coast of the United States.

What color is the sand in Bermuda?

Pink and Beige.

How many Parishes are in Bermuda?

There are Seven Parishes in Bermuda.

Does it snow in Bermuda? If not, why not?

No it doesn't. It does not snow there, because of a jet stream that is off of Bermudas coast seperating it from Africa and Europe.

How many Hurricanes does Bermuda get a year?

On average, Bermuda gets 3-5 hurricanes a year.
